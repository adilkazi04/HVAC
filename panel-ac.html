Panel AC

Our Excellent System Details

Panel air conditioners are specific equipment used along with other machines and electronic systems. One of the requirements for the electronic equipment and machines is to maintain the temperature in which they operate which helps to prevent them from damage due to issues like overheating, increased temperature and so on. The machinery requires regular cooling to ensure less possibility of fault and other problems. Panel ac are used across industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, etc. to break down the heat caused by the machines.

The electronic and electrical components used in the hardware and panels of modern-day applications dissipate a lot of heat and work round the clock continuously without any stoppages irrespective of the location. When these components are exposed to high temperatures, it has an impact on their lifespan and functioning. Hence the need for air conditioning to maintain the temperature optimal for the smooth and sustained functioning of the components is extremely important. This is where panel air conditioners are commonly used and efficiently do the required job. Ultimately the panel air conditioning systems help to maintain the temperature and humidity of the sophisticated environment made up of complex systems.

Some of the features that justify using this type of air conditioners, especially in the industrial space and machinery

  • The ability to remove a high amount of heat in the environment where they operate
  • Evaporator coil and evaporator fan of big size are required when compared to normal room air conditioners
  • The energy efficiency ratio is better due to the big equipment and helps in more energy savings comparatively
  • Panel air conditioners are expected to work throughout the year without any breaks and not for a specific amount of time in a day

Maintaining the productivity of the machines and electronic equipment is mandatory to achieve the best, optimum and accurate results expected. A conducive environment hence becomes an unavoidable requirement in the case of these machines. The side ac panels of the Panel air conditioners help to achieve this objective from a temperature control and maintenance perspective. Additionally, keeping the machines and electronic systems devoid of exposure to any dust and other harmful substances also supports the efficient functioning of the equipment. A clean and controlled atmosphere is what panel air conditioners offer for the electronica and other equipment.

The ac panel set in India are available in numerous designs and specifications depending on the specific requirement. Mounting of the air conditioning systems is also done based on the equipment it is meant to be used along with. Side-mounted variants are a common choice since they provide a uniform and unrestricted flow of air from one end to the other.

Eco-friendly refrigerants used in the panel air conditioning systems. Considering that usage is also for industrial purposes, the guidelines and standards also need to be adhered to strictly to avoid any environmental hazards.

The choice of the model or variant based on specifications would have to be based on the application and purpose. Capacity and design and few of the important aspects to consider during the selection. Considering the wide range of applications mentioned below (where it is used), the appropriate equipment or model needs to be selected and installed with professional help

  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Chemical plants
  • Power generation
  • Telecom
  • Food processing
  • Engineering
