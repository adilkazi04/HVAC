Split AC

Split AC

Our Excellent System Details

A split unit air conditioner is an air conditioning system that consists of two separate units. One of the units is the water or air-cooled condensing unit and the other is the air handling unit. The air handling unit is kept inside the space and the condensing unit is usually kept outdoors. The multi split ac are referred to as split since they separate the conventional air conditioner into two different individual units - one indoor and one outdoor. While the outdoor unit comprises the compressor and condenser equipment, the indoor unit consists of the air filtering and distribution components.

Two units of the multi split air conditioners are connected by insulated refrigerant lines. Refrigerant is dispersed through the copper pipes that pass through the systems to generate cool or heated air. As the compressor unit of the split air conditioning system is kept outdoors, the noise issue is considerably reduced and the space remains relatively quiet with no noise from the equipment. From an energy consumption point of view, split air conditioning systems are considered to be a more efficient option. A good model that is highly star rated can be quite energy-efficient in addition to keeping the environment clean and also reducing power consumption. The split air conditioning system is a good choice when replacing the old air conditioning unit currently installed in any kind of space.

In comparison with the window AC, though the split AC can be more expensive, it consumes much less power which helps in saving cost. One major advantage of the multi split air conditioner in India is the ease of installation especially when there is no duct setup available for the house or any other building space. The additional cost involved in getting the ductwork done can be easily avoided by pursuing the split option. The split unit works well for smaller spaces and rooms providing comfortable cooling and heating. When set up and installed properly, the unit is easy to use and makes the conditioned space quite comfortable.

A notable benefit of the new split air conditioners is the ability to expand and use them for multiple rooms by installing additional air outlets for each room. One outdoor unit can be connected to multiple indoor units. The individual thermostat allows control of the usage by turning the system off and on as required. Each indoor unit can be controlled and set to the desired temperature. The system is usually referred to as multi-split air conditioner installation. From an installation point of view, the only additional requirement is for the copper tubing that connects the outdoor compressor to each air outlet.

Selecting the right size unit is an important aspect when purchasing a split air conditioner. Depending on the size of the room, the appropriate size can be identified and installed. This can be helpful to save on unnecessary costs both with the unit and power consumption in the long run. Doing a bit of research on the energy efficiency and recommended parameters would also be a good idea.

Getting the installation done by an authorized specialist or vendor is also recommended to avoid issues (with operating efficiency, heating and cooling not meeting expectations, better lifespan) that can arise out of improper installation. Regular service of the equipment by the licensed vendors also can be helpful in better functioning and reduce faults proactively.
