Cold storage

Our Excellent System Details

Cold storage is a facility used for commercial purposes to store different types of products that can perish over some time if kept at normal temperature. The focus is primarily around food products that are required to be stored in cold temperatures to remain and usable for a comparatively extended time frame compared to normal circumstances. Some examples of products that require cold storage are fruits, vegetables, fish, milk, meat and so on. These products are stored under controlled conditions to prevent them from getting perished quickly to the effect of normal temperature.

Depending on the requirement, different types of cold storage are used and can be highly classified as

  • Short term storage
  • Long term storage
  • Frozen storage or Freezer
  • Cold storage cooked food

Short-term storage is usually meant in commercial establishments like supermarkets where the products are continuously sold to the customers and the products are expected to be in the storage for a shorter time frame.

Also known as temporary storage, the time frame can be for a maximum period of 2 weeks. Long-term storage could be scenarios wherein the products may need to be kept for a couple of months to maintain their usability. Usually, long-term storage may be used in bigger facilities and establishments. Frozen storage can be for purposes wherein products have to be kept in freezing conditions for an even longer duration and are not generally meant for food products.

The temperature at which the products have to be stored is a crucial factor in determining the type of cold storage required. Depending on the temperature requirement associated with the product being stored, the type of storage that meets the specifications should be selected and used. The various commercial establishments are expected to follow the guidelines and regulations defined by the concerned government authorities when making use of the cold storage, type of products being stored in the same and the temperature at which they are maintained.

Looking at the types of largest cold storage in a different way and their purposes

  • One type is cold storage (E.g. freezers) used by the manufacturers who need it for storing the manufactured products before the products are shipped to the dealers or other vendors
  • Normal vendors like supermarkets and shops require cold storage to keep the products before and during the sales
  • Logistics is another area where cold storage is extensively used to store the products before it reaches the destination

Cold storages are vital to the businesses that are into the manufacture and sale of perishable products. The business establishments may need different types of cold storage depending on the perishable products they deal with, since each product may have a temperature requirement depending on their temperature sensitivity. For e.g., fish and meat products may have a different temperature requirement compared to fruits and vegetables. The rate at which a perishable product would decay could be dependent on the temperature and hence using the right cold storage is extremely important to prevent decay of the product and avoid any damage.

Since the cold storages list offer customized temperature options, it can serve multiple purposes. With the advance of technology, the goal is always to exceed the customer's expectations. Cold storage technology has improved a lot and offers the much-needed flexibility of the customers to monitor and control the quality of the product stored.
