Industrial Oil chillers

Oil chiller

Our Excellent System Details

Industrial Oil chillers are used to maintain the temperature of the different types of oils. Also referred to as oil cooling chillers or oil coolers, they are used to cool oils like hydraulic oil, lubricant oil and other types of oils that are used for industrial purposes. They are specially designed to cool oils and manufactured for oil cooling purposes.

In different types of machines and tools, there are chances of overheating due to various reasons. It could be caused by the bearings and gears getting overheated, temperature rise of hydraulic oil etc. Hence, there is always a need for cooling down the oils used in the machines and tools.

Oil chillers working principals are used in industrial environments for the smooth and trouble-free functioning of various equipment and machines. A chiller provides consistent temperature and pressure to your industrial process. Ensuring there are no temperature and pressure variations in an industrial setup is vital for successful development and manufacture and high-quality products.

Chillers are required to force the oil around the different components to provide lubrication and remove heat caused by friction. The simple working principle of an oil chiller is as below - when the oil chiller is working, the oil pump extracts oil from the oil tank to the evaporator of the cold oil machine to perform heat exchange thereby cooling the oil. The cooled oil is then sent back to the chamber to be mixed with the hot oil.

In general, the different types of oil chillers help to maintain the temperature and thereby help in keeping the associated machines and equipment at optimum working efficiency and also maintain the quality. The oil chillers are used in a wide range of industries like pharmaceuticals, welding, engineering, chemicals, laser technology, plastics, furnaces, electroplating and many more.

Oils when at a high temperature can result in various disadvantages. When the temperature is higher, the viscosity of the oil reduces and the oil becomes thinner. It can lead to leakages resulting in malfunctioning of the equipment and machines. Continuous high temperatures can also affect the quality of the oil that results in the need for frequent replacement.

Different models of oil chillers are available in the market offering a series of advantages

  • Protect the equipment from variation in temperature allowing it to function smoothly
  • Maintaining the viscosity of the oil helps to maintain the stability of the machines
  • Prevent deterioration of the oil quality by controlling the temperature
  • Alarm systems are fitted with the oil chiller to provide warnings in case of any faulty scenario
  • Oil chillers are usually easy to clean, maintain, install and occupy less space
  • Keep the equipment and oil at the same temperature

Using an oil chiller in India of the highest quality and efficient maintenance of the device can be beneficial to ensure the efficient functioning of the different machines used in the industrial processes and establishments.

A recommended industrial oil chiller is certainly a must for your requirements to manage the temperature of the machines and equipment.
