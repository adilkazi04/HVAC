Tower air conditioning systems

Tower AC

Our Excellent System Details

Tower air conditioning systems are models consisting of two units similar to the split air conditioners. One is the indoor unit which is kept on the floor and the other is the outdoor unit which is installed outside the room.

A notable difference between the tower air conditioners and other types is their portability. While the other models are fixed post-installation, the tower model happens to be portable. They are also referred to as floor-standing air conditioners in some cases. Naming or terminology of the tower air conditioning systems is quite synonymous with the appearance of the equipment. The tower air conditioner has a higher cooling capacity and offers more instant cooling in comparison to the other models.

The indoor unit of the tower AC is mobile and quite tall. It stands tall unlike the other models, is not fixed to a wall and occupies space on the floor. Air is absorbed by the indoor unit and then cooled by the refrigerant flow within the evaporator. All the exhausted heat is sent out through the outdoor unit. Most of the tower air conditioner models have air filters fitted within them.

5-ton Tower air conditioning systems also have multiple features like wifi control, child locking and others. Some of the other notable benefits of the tower model are

  • It offers instant cooling with the big advanced unit
  • Well suited for larger spaces since the cooling capacity is higher and well distributed across the big area
  • As they are not permanently mounted at a place, the model is highly portable since it always rests on the floor
  • Looks are quite beautiful and can complement the interiors of the home or any other space
  • The installation procedure is not as complicated as for some of the other variants

    • A few of the disadvantages with tower air conditioning systems are

  • Not fit usually for smaller places and rooms as they occupy space on the floor and have a higher cooling capacity more suitable for large rooms
  • Power consumption is also on the higher side due to bigger cooling capacity
  • The units of the tower air conditioners are comparatively noisy

Tower air conditioners are meant for bigger spaces like shops, commercial establishments that require higher cooling capacity for a larger space and hence not considered to be suitable usually for homely use. The noise factor also plays a part in being unfit for the rooms in the house. Quick cooling and higher capacity make them a perfect fit for bigger spaces where noise may not be a big factor too.

Even though tower air conditioners are not considered to be as efficient as split air conditioning systems, cooling capacity is quite an advantage. Despite all the limitations associated with them, the tower air conditions are a highly recommended and sought-after option for big commercial spaces due to their excellent cooling capacity.

Unless the selection of tower air conditioner is based on specific requirements, the variant is not a commonly recommended one considering some of the disadvantages especially the one around power consumption that can turn out to be a pretty expensive affair in the longer run. Hence, the suggestion would be to decide the air conditioning model based on the situation and need.
